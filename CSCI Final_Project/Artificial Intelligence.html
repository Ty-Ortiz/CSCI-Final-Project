Artificial Intelligence



The term "artificial intelligence" is credited to John McCarthy who is known as one of the founding fathers of artificial intelligence. Research into ai had officially begun in 1956 and the group of researchers had found many things and one of those things was that the human brain was too complicated to be replicated by the program the machine runs and the knowledge needed to create that program hadn't reached that point. They had done many other test to try and improve the ai but with only partial results. The group also came up with the idea of ai have the capabilities to self-improve with the idea of the machines using self-modifying code to make itself smarter allowing the machine to grow in intelligence and improve its calculation speeds. Ai had progressed tremendously in the past few years being able to create images, videos, and voices with it only improving each day that passes. Many people are scared of the future ai holds and that isn't a recent fear but something that has been around for decades with people even creating works of fiction showing the danger that ai could pose with two notable examples being I Have No Mouth, And I Must Scream and The Terminator series.
