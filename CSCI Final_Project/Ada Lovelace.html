Ada Lovelace



Ada Lovelace was a european mathematician who is widely considered to be one of the first computer scientists. While working with Charles Babbage on his analytical engine, the first mechanical computer, she had designed the first computer algorithm which was able to computate Bernoulli numbers. These are a series of numbers that can be defined by the Taylor series expansions of other mathematical formulas. Although she did create the algorithm she wasn't able to live to see the analytical engine but she had made many contributions towards its creation.
